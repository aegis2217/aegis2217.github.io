About the Project

  • MASK-UP: Mask Is A Must! is a nonprofit project organized by five (5) Grade 12 students from Philippine Science High School ‚Äì Central Mindanao Campus (PSHS-CMC), which aims to educate and raise awareness on the proper usage of face masks. The name of the project is derived from current problems with the usage like (1) wearing a face mask below the nose and (2) not wearing a face mask at all. As part of the masses, it is our responsibility to cooperate and adhere to the standard safety and health protocols for the betterment and welfare of the general public. This project will focus mainly on PSHS-CMC students as the beneficiaries since the project organizers believe that starting in PSHS-CMC can propagate information to the general public.
